CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Basilio
  • The eldest son of Sisa and Pedro. He is 10 years of age.
  • He has a little brother named Crispin.
  • He was hired by Crisostomo Ibarra to be his servant.
Sisa
  • The mother and caretaker of Basilio and Crispin.
  • She is a victim of domestic abuse from her husband, Pedro.
  • Even though she and her family is poor, she tries her best to provide for her children that she loves very much.
Crispin
  • He is 7 years of age.
  • He has an older brother named Basilio. His parents are Sisa and Pedro.
  • He was accused of taking two pieces of gold. And for punishment, he was taken by the Sacristan Mayor.
Pedro
  • Pedro was seen as a prominent abuser to his wife, Sisa.
  • He abuses his entire household. He is also depicted as someone who is very lazy.
  • Instead of saving money he uses his family's earnings to gamble.
Padre Salvi
  • He was established as the vice priest of San Diego.
  • He is corrupt in showcasing his rules across society.
  • His rival is Alferez.
